## Angular Momentum of the Whole Rotating Rigid Solid

$$L_{solid}^\to = L_1^\to + L_2^\to+...$$
$$where \space L_n = r_np_1\sin(90\degree)$$
$$L_n = r_n * m_n * v_n$$
$$L_n = m_n r_n^2w$$

```ad-important
SINCE their directions are all the same due to right-hand rule
$$L_{solid} = Iw$$
```
