# Indeterminate Forms

```ad-important
To solve indeterminate forms of the remainding types, utilize the following:
$$\lim_{x \to 0} x^x = \lim_{x \to 0} e^{ln(x^x)} = e^{\lim_{x \to 0} xln(x)}$$
```
